Brand as product

The identity is not decoration sitting next to the app, it is the app's language. The caliper is the measure action, cyan is the live reading, and the verdict colors are the output. A judge sees the logo and already understands the pitch: measure the site, return a verdict.

That is the answer to the "Google Maps clone" zero. The brand itself says you measure what a map cannot.

06

Alternatives from the naming study, all drawn from the same well as Sondar: remote surveying, measure-from-scan. The instrument words drop straight into this caliper-and-cyan system. Scored on product-fit, premium-technical feel, and viability. Tap a domain to copy.

The call

Datum for gravitas — it sounds like a measurement company, not an app. Sextant for story — the remote-measurement instrument, and it locks to the caliper mark. But Sondar still wins on meaning: it is the literal verb for what the product does, and nobody owns it.
